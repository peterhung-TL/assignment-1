Inspiration

'remember a stranger' is the debut full-length album by Singapore shoegaze band motifs. Recorded in early 2022 by Leonard Soosay of Snakeweed Studios, the album straddles two themes - 'fading memories' and 'coping with loss'. Many of the songs were inspired by Elspeth Ong's memories of her growing up years and experiences. The album's cover art draws parallel with this concept by capturing the 'nostalgia of childhood' through the experimentation with various colours and textures to generate a 'hazy vintage' effect.

Who Are We?

motifs is a shoegaze/dream pop band that formed in Singapore in 2019. The band comprises Elspeth Ong (vocals, guitar), JJ Tan (lead guitar), Paul Yuen (bass), Badrul Amin (synth/vocals/guitar) and Jolin Chiam (drums). With the influences from 90s shoegaze, post-punk, and dream-pop acts, the band creates a world of delicate soundscapes with the combination of lilting melodies with reverb-drenched nostalgia.
